GMap Instructions

  • Node Management:
    • Add Node: Go to the "Node" tab, enter a name, description, size, and color, then click "Add Node".
    • Update Node: Select a node on the graph, modify its properties in the "Node" tab, and click "Update Node". You can also rename a node here.
    • Remove Node: Select a node and click "Remove Node" in the "Node" tab.
  • Connection Management:
    • Add Connection: Go to the "Connection" tab, enter "From Node" and "To Node" IDs, select a color, and click "Add Connection".
    • Quick Connect: Hold Ctrl, click the "From" node, then click the "To" node.
    • Remove Connection: Enter "From Node" and "To Node" IDs in the "Connection" tab and click "Remove Connection".
  • General Actions:
    • Delete Selected (Keyboard): Select a node or connection and press the Delete key to remove it.
    • Quick Remove (Mouse): Hold Ctrl + Alt and click on a node or connection to remove it directly.
    • Search Node: Use the search bar at the top-left to find and focus on a specific node.
  • Settings & Project:
    • Dark Mode: Toggle dark mode in the "Settings" tab.
    • Export/Import Project: Use the "Project" tab to save or load your graph data.
    • Sidebar Toggle: Use the `>` or `<` button to hide/show the sidebar.
